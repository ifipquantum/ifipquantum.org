Meeting in Munich

The yearly meeting of the WG was hosted at TU Munich, satellite to the Munich Quantum Software Forum.

Ina Shaeffer and Tobias Osborne step down as Acting Chair and Acting Secretary. We thank them for their efforts in setting up the WG. Aleks Kissinger has taken over as Chair of the WG and Luis Barbosa as Secretary. See People.
